📦 RRC packaging design72% / 100%▼
+10pp vs 2018 · 28pp gap to target

RRC = Reusable, Recyclable, or Compostable. Measures what % of packaging is designed to be recovered — based on material/format, not actual recycling outcomes.

⚠️ A bottle can be "recyclable" but still land in a landfill without infrastructure. 2025 target: 100%.

Elimination means completely removing plastic packaging — not recycling it after use.

✅ Best possible action. 775,000t ≈ 55 billion plastic bottles never produced.
